Erica Hayden (Knight) is a young expectant mother who is wrongly implicated in a crime and sent to prison for five years. Erica learns that she must find a guardian for her daughter or she will become a ward of the state. The prisons warden, Frank Cole (Duggan), becomes infatuated with Erica and effectively blocks her chances for a parole. When another inmates child dies, the woman becomes deranged. Erica prevents a tragedy and earns the right to be freed to be with her daughter, while the Warden Coles unethical methods come to light.
House of Women
1962
