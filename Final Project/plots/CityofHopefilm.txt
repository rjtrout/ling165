The film tells the story of Nick Rinaldi (Vincent Spano), who has spent his life in one New Jersey city, getting a free ride from his well-connected father (Tony LoBianco) and hearing the locals talk of his brothers death in Vietnam. As Rinaldi searches for more self-control, he quits the contractors job provided by his father, feeling that major events are about to happen in his life. By the films ending, his life will change, as will the lives of many others.
