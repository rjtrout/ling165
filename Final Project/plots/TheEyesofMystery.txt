As described in a film magazine, Carma Carmichael (Storey), who lives with her uncle Quincy Carmichael (Andrews), is kidnapped by her father and held for ransom. In order to trap the criminals and secure Jack Carrington (Barker) as Carmas husband, Quincy fakes his death and makes Jack his heir. Carma is angered by her uncles action is determined to take her rightful place. By going through some of her uncles papers, she discovers that the man she believes to be her father is an impostor and that her father is dead. Carmas supposed father and a group of moonshiners attack the Carmichael home and are fought off by Carma, Jack, and a friend. Quincy, believing it is time to return to life, does so in time to get the sheriffs posse on the house grounds, drive off the moonshiners, and capture the crooks.
