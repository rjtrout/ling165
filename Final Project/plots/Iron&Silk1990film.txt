Mark Salzman always was interested in Kung-Fu and the Chinese culture, claims to have seen every Kung-Fu movie. 1982, with a degree in Chinese literature, he visits a Hunan province Medical university in China for two years to teach Chinese teachers the English language. He learns the refinements of correct behavior among Chinese people, makes friends with his pupils, falls in love with the young doctor Ming, learns Chinese Martial Art, WuShu (similar Kung-Fu) from the famous teacher Pan... but also learns about political repression, especially when hes forbidden contact with some of his friends.
