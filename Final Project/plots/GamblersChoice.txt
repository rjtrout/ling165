The professional gambler Ross Hadley is the owner of a posh gaming establishment in the heart of New York. Hadleys main antagonist is his childhood friend Mike McGlennon. McGlennon, now a police lieutenant, is determined to stop the gambling activities of Hadley. Hadleys and McGlennnons relationship becomes more complex, when they notice, that they both are in love with the attractive Mary Hayes. Mary sings in nightclubs under the stage name Vi Parker.
Gambler's Choice
