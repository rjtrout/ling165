Harry Quatermain (George Montgomery) is the son of Allan Quatermain who first set out on the quest for the source of Solomons wealth, and he is determined to succeed where his father failed. He goes to Africa with his good friend Rick Cobb (David Farrar) and as they continue on their journey, Erica Neuler (Taina Elg) joins them. She is the daughter of a missionary who has been killed by a local tribe. Harry cannot hide his antagonism toward Erica. She is German, and Harrys mother was killed at sea by Germans in World War I.
