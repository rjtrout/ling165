Pyotr Drozdov, a high-ranking official from Moscow, comes to the provincial museum of a half-forgotten classic author of Russian literature at the request of the governor of the region, who wants to take the land from the museum and build a new residence on it. And at first Drozdov supports this decision, but acquaintance with Masha, a literary critic working in the museum as deputy director, changes his view not only on this problem, but in general for his whole life ...
Two Days
2003
