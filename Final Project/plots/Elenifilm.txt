The film is told in a flashback format with Gage, now living in the United States, returning to his native Greece to solve the mystery of his mothers death when he was a child. The film looks back to the effect of the 1940s Greek Civil War on the remote Greek village of his upbringing, and he investigates what happened to his mother after Communist guerrillas of ΔΣΕ invade the village.
