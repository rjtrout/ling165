The Stooges are exterminators mistaken for B.O. Pictures publicity department. They are then instructed to drum up publicity for the studios lovely new actress Dolly Devore (Christine McIntyre), and arrange a fake kidnapping. However, two gangsters hear the Stooges plan and kidnap Devore for real. The gangsters break into her hotel room, then tied her hands behind her back and zip her up in a large garment bag, forcing the Stooges to come to her rescue.
