Howard Wakefield (Cranston) seems to have it all, with a successful job, beautiful family and house in the suburbs, until one day he simply vanishes. Unbeknownst to his wife (Garner), he is still at home, but is hiding in the attic above their separate garage. From his hiding place, he secretly studies the lives of his wife, his twin daughters, and their neighbors, but discovers more about himself.
