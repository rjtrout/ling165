Kansas, 1910: In town trying to sell player pianos, traveling saleslady Agatha Knabenshu doesnt have much luck. She lends $1,000 to struggling inventor Bertram Webb, whose new invention, a cow-milking machine, promptly causes $1,500 in damages. With heartless banker Hubert Shelton about to foreclose on the Webb familys home, Bert has one last hope, a wood-burning automobile that hes created. At an auto race with a $1,500 first prize, Bert is unable to drive, so Agatha gets behind the wheel in his place and wins the race.
