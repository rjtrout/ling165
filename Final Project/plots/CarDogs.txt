The story is set at the Chamberlain Auto Group which is run by Malcolm Chamberlain (played by Chris Mulkey). He has his son Mark working for him as the GM. Mark (played by Patrick J. Adams) is also the general manager. Malcolm is a ruthless and manipulative car dealer. He offers his son a major part of a new store if he and the team of car salesmen can move 35 cars in a day, and they have to do it by 5pm. The sales manager (played by Josh Hopkins) has his eyes on the new dealership for himself and is playing dirty tricks behind Marks back.
Car Dogs
2017
