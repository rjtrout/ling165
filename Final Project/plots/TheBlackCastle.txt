Sir Ronald Burton (Greene), a British gentleman, investigates the disappearance of two of his friends at the Austrian estate of the sinister Count von Bruno (McNally). Bruno secretly seeks revenge against the leaders of a British force that set the natives against him in colonial Africa: Burtons missing friends are among Brunos victims, and Burton is now also in the trap. Burton plans to escape with Brunos abused Countess, but the Counts henchmen bar the way.
