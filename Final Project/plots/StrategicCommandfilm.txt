Rick Harding (Dudikoff) is a former Marines officer, now working as biological weapons scientist for the FBI. The movie starts, with Hardings lab being infiltrated, which results with terrorist having their hands on a deadly nerve warfare chemical called Bromax 36.Led by Carlos Gruber (Norton), the terrorists hijack the plane of the Vice-President, a Boeing 747-200 from Los Angeles to Washington D.C.. Harding must participate in a midair effort to retake the 747 and save the Vice President and his wife.
