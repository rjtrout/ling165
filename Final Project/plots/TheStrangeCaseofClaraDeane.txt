A young dress designer marries an insurance agent. They soon have a daughter, But what the wife doesnt know is that her husband is actually a criminal, who soon involves her—unwittingly—in robbery. Sentenced to prison, she gives up her baby for adoption. When she is released 15 years later, she set out to find her long-lost daughter. A police inspector get involved in her search and, for reasons of his own, tries to dissuade her from finding her child.
The Strange Case of Clara Deane
1932
