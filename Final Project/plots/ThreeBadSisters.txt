Valerie, Vicki and Lorna Craig are the daughters of a man worth $40 million who is killed in a Wyoming plane crash. The pilot, Jim Norton, was unharmed. Jim is unable to fly while the crash is investigated. Valerie offers him $200,000 to seduce and abandon her sister Lorna, executor of their fathers will. Lorna is engaged to George Gurney, their fathers attorney. Jim finds her contemplating a leap off a high cliff into the water, dared by Valerie to do it. Valerie threatens to frame Jim for murdering her father if he refuses to cooperate. Then sister Vicki makes romantic advances, trying to lure Jim away from her sisters clutches. Valerie lashes her with a whip, scarring her face. Vicki flees in a car, crashes it and is killed. Jim proposes marriage to Lorna, who isnt sure if she can trust him. Valerie attempts to murder Lorna and make it look like an accident. When her scheme fails, Valerie, too, is killed in a speeding car. Jim goes to the cliff and discovers Lorna has made the leap. He dives into the water and saves her.
Three Bad Sisters
1956
