Dolph wakes up early in the morning in his suburban home to find that his dog is missing. He is in denial that he has lost his job and shows up to his office to work every day and pretends to do his work. Dolphs need to live in a world of denial about all the areas in his life that are obviously not working is found throughout his life. A palm tree in his backyard transforms into a pine tree. Its raining inside Dolphs office even though none of his co-workers seem to notice it or care that theyre being soaked. To put his life back together, he goes in search for his dog.
Wrong (film)
2013
