Glee: The 3D Concert Movie depicts a Glee Cast concert in East Rutherford, New Jersey during the groups Glee Live! In Concert! tour. It features behind-the-scenes footage, and a setlist of songs from the shows first and second seasons. As well as the behind-the-scenes footage, the film portrays the series influence on teenagers, including one who is gay named Trenton, one with Asperger syndrome named Josey Pickering, and a cheerleader with short stature named Janae. The fan segments were created by documentary filmmaker Jennifer Arnold.
