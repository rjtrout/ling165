The wife, Ellen (Susannah York), of an international industrialist (Robert Culp) and her two children are kidnapped from their Athens home by a terrorist group and taken to an abandoned monastery on an imposing, needle-shaped island. Jim McCabe (James Coburn), Ellens ex-husband hires a crew of professional hang gliders to help him rescue her and the kids from the terrorists mountain top lair.
Sky Riders
1976
