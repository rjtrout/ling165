Sea captain Jim Marsden is about to be hanged for a murder he didnt commit, and is rescued from the gallows by two of his crewmen. To clear the captains name, they head for the island of Pulinan, where they believe the real murderer is hiding. During the search for the killer, one thing leads to another and Jim and the crew soon find that their troubles have just started. Investigating a possible hiding place of the killer, Jim encounters huge sea monsters in Haunted Harbor.
