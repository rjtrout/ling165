After trying to save a mortally wounded detectives life, Dr. Condon flees for his own life because of gangster Jim Cravens intention to eliminate him, an eyewitness to the crime. Condon moves to another town and changes his name. He finds lodging in the remote home of the wheelchair-using Colonel Maitland and daughter Laurie. Craven comes to town, is recognized by a deputy and shoots him. Condon must reveal his true identity as a doctor to operate on the law officer. He is at Cravens mercy, but Colonel Maitlands deadeye aim with a rifle saves his life.
You Have to Run Fast
1961
