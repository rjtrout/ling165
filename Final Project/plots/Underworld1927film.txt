Boisterous gangster kingpin Bull Weed rehabilitates the down-and-out Rolls Royce Wensel, a former lawyer who has fallen into alcoholism. The two become confidants, with Rolls Royces intelligence aiding Weeds schemes, but complications arise when Rolls Royce falls for Weeds girlfriend Feathers McCoy. Adding to Weeds troubles are attempts by a rival gangster, Buck Mulligan, to muscle in on his territory. Their antagonism climaxes with Weed killing Mulligan and he is imprisoned, awaiting a death sentence. Rolls Royce devises an escape plan, but he and Feathers face a dilemma, wondering if they should elope together and leave Bull Weed to his fate.
