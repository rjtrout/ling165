Bill Higgins (Steven Cooke), a high school senior who longs to score a date with cheerleader Krissi (Lezlie Z. McCraw), discovers an alcoholic leprechaun in a beer bottle. The leprechaun, named Lepkey, must grant Bill three wishes before hes allowed to return to Ireland. Bills wishes end up causing more trouble due to Lepkeys alcoholism and diminished magical skills.
Getting Lucky
