Danny and Bernie are two single men who live in Chicago. When Danny meets Debbie at Mother Malones ("Mothers"), a bar in the Chicago Gold Coast, the two start a relationship from a one-night stand. The film follows the couple for the first year of their relationship: their meeting after a softball game, her moving in with him, mutual friction at Thanksgiving, their breakup on New Years Eve, his apology and declaration of love on St. Patricks Day, and their reconciliation at a softball game.
