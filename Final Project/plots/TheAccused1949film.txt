Wilma Tuttle (Young) is a college professor who arouses the sexual interest of her student Bill Perry (Douglas Dick). When Perry tries to rape Tuttle, she beats him to death with a tire iron. She covers up her crime by making it seem as though Perry was killed while diving into the sea from a precipitous cliff. As she follows the police investigation of Perrys death, Wilma realizes that shell never be able to escape her conscience, especially when she falls in love with Warren Ford (Cummings), the dead boys guardian.
