Bonny Kane and Skid Johnson are vaudeville performers in the 1920s. The two of them suffer marital difficulties when Skid gets an offer to appear on Broadway while Bonny gets left behind on the road. Things get worse with Skids increasing drinking problem and the fact that the press has reported him to be spending a lot of time with his pretty co-star.
