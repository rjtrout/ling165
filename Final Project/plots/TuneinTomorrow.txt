Martin Loader works at the local radio station, that just hired a new scriptwriter, Pedro Carmichael. Martins Aunt Julia, not related by blood, returns home after many years away and Martin falls for her. Once Pedro finds out about this romance, he starts incorporating details of it into the script of his daily drama series. Soon, Martin and Julia are not only hearing about their fictional selves over the radio, but they hear about what they are going to do next.
Tune in Tomorrow
1990
