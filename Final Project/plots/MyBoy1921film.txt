Seven-year-old Jackie Blair (Coogan), the son of poor immigrants, is orphaned and, leaving Ellis Island, finds his way into the home of a hardbitten ex-seaman, Captain Bill. Despite his initial misgivings, the Captain grows fond of the boy. Tension mounts when officials attempt to take Jackie away for deportation, and the Captain tries to find a way to keep him safe.
