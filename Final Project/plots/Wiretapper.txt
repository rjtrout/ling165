Jim Vaus returns from the war and marries Alice. He struggles to make a living. He was hired by Charles Rumsden to fix a doorbell, and realizes that his client is a mob boss. Alice discovers the source of Jims income and their relationship was expired and strained to the breaking point. She forces Jim to attend a Billy Grahams Los Angeles Crusade, in her attempt to save their marriage and Jims soul.
