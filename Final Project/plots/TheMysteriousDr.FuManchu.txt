A young white girl, Lia Eltham, is left in Fu Manchus care. A British regiment, chasing Boxer rebels, fires on Fu Manchus home, killing his wife and child. When Lia Eltham grows up, he uses her as an instrument for revenge, killing all descendants of those who killed his wife. Opposing Fu Manchu are Police Inspector Nayland Smith and Dr. Jack Petrie.
The Mysterious Dr. Fu Manchu
1929
