Joe Linwood (Ted DiBiase) of the US Marine Force Recon attempts to rescue his wife Robin (Lara Cox), and other guests from a hotel which has been taken over by a gang of bloodthirsty terrorists. The Linwoods are vacationing at the lavish Thailand resort when the terrorists invade during the hotels grand opening. Joe escapes the initial onslaught and must find a way to save his wife and the other hostages.
