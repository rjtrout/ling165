After being told by his ranch boss Lynn that she plans to sell her ranch to finance a move east, Jack is framed for cattle rustling by Barnes, the man set to buy the ranch. With the help of his pal Buzz, Jack escapes the noose and the two set out to clear Jacks name. In addition, Jack is suspicious of the reason why Barnes wants to own Lynns ranch in the first place.
