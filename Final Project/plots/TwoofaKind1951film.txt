Mike Farrell (OBrien) is induced by con artists Brandy Kirby (Scott) and attorney Vincent Mailer (Knox) to purloin a rich couples ten million dollar estate by having Farrell pose as their long-lost son. When the old man refuses to change his will, Mailer decides to kill them, and Kirby plays along. Farrell refuses to assist, and Mailer plans to kill him too. After a botched attempt, with Kirbys help, Farrell exposes the scam to the old man, dooming Mailers plan, and allowing Kirby and Farrell to unite, as Two of a Kind.
