The film interlaces two stories set fifty years apart, switching frequently between them. Each tells the story of a childs quest. In 1927, Rose (Simmonds) runs away from her fathers New Jersey home to find her mother/idol, the actress Lillian Mayhew (Moore). In 1977, recently orphaned Ben (Fegley) runs away from his Minnesota home in search of his father.
