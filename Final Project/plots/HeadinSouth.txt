As described in a film magazine, a forest ranger known only as Headin South (Fairbanks) goes forth in search of Spanish Joe (Campeau), a Mexican responsible for most of the treachery and outlawry along the U.S.-Mexican boarder. Headin South gains quite a reputation as he goes along and finally believes himself worthy of joining Joes band. in a whirlwind finish in which Joe is captured, Headin South meets one of Joes near victims (MacDonald) and falls in love with her.
Headin' South
