Judge Ross, on the Federal Bench, rules in favor of a large company in litigation before him, unaware that a smaller company in which he owns considerable stock has been subsumed by the larger firm, thus creating appearance of a conflict of interests. When one of the Judges enemies plots to ruin the Judge over this apparent improper behavior, Judge Rosss daughter Shirley sets out to prove her fathers innocence.
