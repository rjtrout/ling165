The film follows four storylines about each of the couples, titled: "The Mamas Boy" vs. "The Single Mom" "The Non-Committer" vs. "The Girl Who Wants the Ring" "The Dreamer" vs. "The Woman Who Is Her Own Man" "The Player" vs. "The 90 Day Rule Girl" Each of the women are readers of Steve Harveys book Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man. When the men learn that the women are hooked on Harveys advice, they try to turn the tables on their mates, which later seems to backfire.
