Dr. Ernst Grood has succeeded in winning control over the planet Ergro as the first step in his desired conquest of the Universe. Reporter Rex Barrow, his photographer Tim Johnson, Professor Edmund Dorn and his daughter Ella are all captured by Grood, who plans to make use of the professors knowledge. With the help of the professors inventions, Rex is able to free Ergro of Groods domination, while Grood is sent on an endless voyage into space.
