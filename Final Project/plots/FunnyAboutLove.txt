New York cartoonist Duffy Bergman marries gourmet chef Meg Lloyd. Meg wants to have a baby. Duffy agrees, but after unsuccessful attempts, Duffy encourages her to focus on her career and come back to the child issue later. After his mothers death, however, Duffy becomes fixated on wanting to have a child. Meg no longer sees this as a priority, as shes trying to open her own restaurant.
