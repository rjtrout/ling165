Chief Pawnee was brought up a white man named Pale Arrow. Wagon trains began to invade Pawnees land, and the Chief sends the Pale Arrow to the white people. Pale Arrow takes the name Paul Fletcher and becomes a scout of the wagon train. The Chief wants to live in peace with white people. But after his death, Crazy Fox becomes the Chief of Pawnee and leads Pawnee to attack that wagon train.
