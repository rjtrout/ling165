Mason is summoned to the Laxter mansion in the dead of night to write granddaughter Wilma out of invalid Peter Laxters will, to keep her from marrying suspected fortune hunter Doug. Peter dies in a mysterious fire and Laxters two grandsons, Sam Laxter and Frank Oafley, inherit his estate on the condition old caretaker Schuster and his cat Clinker are kept on. When cat-hating Sam threatens Clinker, Perry steps in and learns Laxters death was suspicious and the family fortune and diamonds are missing.
