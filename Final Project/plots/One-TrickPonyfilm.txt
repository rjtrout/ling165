Paul Simon plays Jonah Levin, a once-popular folk-rock musician who hasnt had a hit in ten years, and now opens for bands like the B-52s (Levins bandmates are played by musicians Steve Gadd, Eric Gale, Tony Levin, and Richard Tee, all of whom similarly backed Simon on One-Trick Ponys soundtrack album). Hes trying to record a new album, but faces a number of obstacles, including an indifferent record-company executive (Rip Torn) whos pressuring him to create a hit record with the help of a trendy producer (Lou Reed). Jonah is also trying to restore his relationship with his estranged wife, Marion (Blair Brown), and young son, Matty (Michael Pearlman).
