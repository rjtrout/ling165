Tired of being hit and hurt, football star Marty Reid decides to quit Sanford Colleges team. His buddy Honey Smith understands, but teammate Ed Kirby is so angry, he calls Reid a coward. Reid schemes to have campus coed Pat Carlyle make a play for Reid, coaxing him to return to the field of play. Reid does, but fumbles when he discovers hes been tricked, then gets into a fistfight with Kirby in the locker room. Convinced now his rival isnt yellow, Kirby invites Reid to go back to the field and win the big game.
