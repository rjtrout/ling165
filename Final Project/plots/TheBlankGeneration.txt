Directors and producers Ivan Kral and No Wave filmmaker Amos Poe use behind-the-scenes footage of punk musicians before they became icons. The film includes footage of Iggy Pop, Blondie, Patti Smith, The Ramones, Television, Talking Heads, New York Dolls, The Heartbreakers, The Shirts, and Robert Gordon as visitors to New Yorks CBGBs, Film locations include Maxs Kansas City, The Bottom Line, Bowery, and the Lower East Side.
The Blank Generation
1976
