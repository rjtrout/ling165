Prior to a major fashion show, the people involved find themselves embroiled in their own personal battles. Lorenzo Mancini is a famous Slavic designer who learns that he is dying of cancer. With this knowledge, he attempts to make amends with his former wife, Irene, and his lover Guido. In addition, he uses his time to try his very best to convince his son Mario to merge their family business with the hippity-hop fashion industry.
