James "Jamie" Roberts (played by singer, songwriter Rick Springfield), being a pop idol, is used to having his way with women. He meets child psychologist Diana Lawson (Janet Eilber) in a car accident, however, who not only doesnt swoon at his attentions, but has also never heard of him. He tries to win her affection, but its complicated by the fact that his ex-lover, Nicky Nides (Patti Hansen), is still a member of his band.
