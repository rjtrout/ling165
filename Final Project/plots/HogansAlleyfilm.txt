Although he wins the championship by a knockout, prizefighter Lefty OBrien is not a happy man because he broke his left hand on the jaw of his opponent, who ended up seriously hurt. Lefty has a girlfriend, Patsy, but her father is opposed to their getting married. When she is treated for an injury by Dr. Franklin, he also attempts to sweep her off her feet. Lefty and her dad need to come to her rescue when shes trapped on a runaway train.
